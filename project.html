Purify

A game that tells the story of how the brave warrior saves the cursed kingdom with his friends.
You will need to recruit warriors, wizards and other powerful companions to fight against the enemies and reach the destination that needs to be purified. You will always need to decide how to manage your resources like gold and mana. "With great power comes great responsibility." Take care warriors and it's time to save the kingdom.
